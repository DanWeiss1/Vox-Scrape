MH17 is the deadliest aviation incident since 9/11

    Updated by Joseph Stromberg

    on July 17, 2014, 6:34 p.m. ET


    
    

Ray Wert points out a striking fact about the MH17 crash:
Today’s Malaysia Airlines crash is the world's deadliest aviation incident since 9/11. http://t.co/053rTYZfAn



It's true: the 295 people killed in the attack on MH17 are the most of any incident since the 2,996 casualties caused by the September 11 attacks.
